Easily integrate add-to-cart functionality from your Sign Customiser account with WooCommerce.

== Description ==
This is a free plugin to sync the products generated by the embedded Sign Customiser app on your Woocommerce store to your cart.

Managing your account and all data associated with Sign Customiser is done through the [web admin](https://web.signcustomiser.com/login).
